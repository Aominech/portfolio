Hello

I'd say I'm in love with software development. Developing new software or working on an already existing one is an exhilarating feeling. Watching it all come to life is just amazing.

Technology without a doubt is one of the driving forces of the world in recent times and it is certainly bound to be in the years to come. Software development is one of the key components of the industry. developers are tasked with working round the clock to kep up wuth the cerrent needs. Every day new software is conceived somewhere in the world. One of the biggest challenges to software development as has been seen in the past, is the public acceptance of new ideas. Technology revolutionizes how people do their things and thus over the years people have been reluctant to accept the new tech. Big leaps in tech usually seem to be far fetched ideas that wont succeed. However, learning from history I'm hopefull that the new software being developed will find a better acceptance in the world and that we developers will keep being part of the cogs that move the wheels to advance humanity forward.

One cannot underestimate the burden placed on the shoulders of developers, computer scientists, data scientists, software engineers and programmers. A responsibility to deliver and keep humanity on its toes. I believe it is a challenge to predict the future, see what it holds and try to meet that need using the resources available today. From a time when computers seemed to be an impossibility, now we stand on top of the hill as we look towards even greater peaks in the field. From quantum computing to AI and Robotics that will in a similar manner to classical computers revolutionise society. Software development is alive and evident all around us. From finance; block chains, NFTs , fintech, etc : security; forensic gait analysis, biometric analysis and so much more. It remains our duty to keep working away at making life easier and better while safeguarding the essentials of humanity for generations to come.
